Adventure Awaits

With its rich mix of traditions, festivals and landscapes, India will keep your memories illuminated even after stepping out of its shores.

Diverse Landscapes

Kashmir

Snowy glaciers, pristine lakes, picturesque mountains, green meadow, gushing rivers everything about Kashmir is supremely beautiful.

Kerala

Its lush unspoiled tropical beauty, exotic locales and tranquil backwaters attract travelers from across the world who seeks leisurely vacation.

Rajasthan

A profusion of colour, vast deserts, opulent palaces and ancient fortresses, spices piled high in the market, Rajasthan is a photographer’s dream.

Varanasi

Considered the most sacred city in India, Varanasi can turn the atheist into a believer.

North East

From Assam to Arunachal Pradesh, the northeastern states are rich in natural resources, flora, and fauna.
